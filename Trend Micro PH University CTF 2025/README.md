My first CTF. I joined during summer of my junior year in college along with a bunch of my friends.

I was very interested in learning how this stuff worked, so I learned how to install Ubuntu and Linux commands.

I watched tutorials of how to solve different CTFs on OverTheWire and picoCTF to practice.

I taught my friends all of the stuff I learned in online meetings.

We placed 84th out of 199 teams that year. It was a great result considering we had like a month of training.

Since then, I've joined other contests and explored parts of the IT world outside of cybersecurity.

I've come to realize that I don't enjoy them as much as I do this. This is what I want to spend my time on. This is what I really want to understand as if it were the back of my palm. I want 2 git gud.
